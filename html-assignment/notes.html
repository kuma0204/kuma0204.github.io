Paragraphs
For adding a paragraph of text we can use this. The browser will add a space before and after the content of the paragraph
Emphasis
a string of emphasized, slightly more important text. Screen readers will change their voice for this text
Blockquote
a large, stand alone quote from another source.
Highlighting text
used to highlight a piece of text for reference. The keywords in a search results page, the current navigation item.
Line Break
creates a line break that's significant to the content. Useful in poems and addresses where the division of lines is important.
